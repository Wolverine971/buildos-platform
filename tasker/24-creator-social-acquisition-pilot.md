# 24 — Creator Social Acquisition Pilot

**Priority:** P1 — distribution + activation execution
**Created:** 2026-07-10
**Owner:** DJ Wayne
**Campaign:** Writer / Cold Manuscript first; YouTuber second
**Operating home:** [`../docs/marketing/campaigns/creator-acquisition/README.md`](../docs/marketing/campaigns/creator-acquisition/README.md)

## Objective

Run one complete 30-day acquisition loop from creator conversation to seven-day return.

This tracker is not complete when posts are drafted. It is complete when real Writer-facing assets ship, real writers enter the product, attribution is visible, and the return behavior is reviewed.

## Stop Rule

Do not begin with another general algorithm sweep, giant influencer list, or daily queue.

Research is allowed only when it directly produces:

- an executed touch;
- a verified relationship target;
- a creator setup invitation;
- a verbatim language capture;
- a bounded content/format experiment.

Carry no more than three unexecuted social items between sessions.

## Activation Coordination

Coordinate with [`22-activation-as-strategy-assessment.md`](22-activation-as-strategy-assessment.md). The social pilot specifically needs:

- audience and campaign preserved in attribution;
- first real capture observed;
- useful structure/recognition event observed;
- project return within seven days observed;
- product friction captured from setup sessions.

Do not block the first fieldwork conversations on perfect analytics. Use a manual participant ledger while instrumentation is repaired.
