## Question

> What makes you an exceptionally great fit for our talent dense team? (required)

## Answer

Jon Chua pointed me at Red Rover, and I trust Jon's read on the teams worth joining.

Most of my career has been on small teams where engineers think like product people, partner with whoever is closest to the problem, and own features end to end. At Curri I rebuilt the ops console our service-ops team used all day and owned the integration layer to Lyft, Uber, DoorDash, and four regional partners. The work that mattered most wasn't the code, it was sitting with ops and figuring out which signal to surface and when.

Straight on the stack: I write TypeScript every day. My C# is real but rusty, I haven't touched it in years, so there's a ramp.

The deeper reason: I have kids, and I see how much teachers and school admins carry for other people's families. A lot of why I work is to make life easier for the people who carry an unreasonable amount on behalf of others.
