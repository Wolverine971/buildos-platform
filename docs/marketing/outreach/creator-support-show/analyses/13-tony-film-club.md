## Colin & Samir's diagnosis

1. **Format engine = "The film so [blank] it [blanked]."** 20 videos in this format, not just 1.

3. **Thumbnail clarity** — subject on left, info gap on right, consistent.
4. **VHS / grainy aesthetic layer** as recurring visual identity.
