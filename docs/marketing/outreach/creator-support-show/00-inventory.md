## Why this matters

The Creator Support Show is Colin & Samir's flagship pod (now branded under the new `@creatorsupportshow` handle, formerly `@creatorsupportpod`). Per the persona research, every guest-featured episode is a hand-raised mid-tier creator who publicly admits they need workflow / strategy help. **These are perfect BuildOS outreach targets.**

Two classes of episode:

1. **Guest-featured ("Fixing X" + named-guest profiles)** — single creator on the couch, problem-solving format. Direct outreach value.
2. **General show (Colin & Samir commentary)** — broader topics like "Did MrBeast ruin YouTube?", "Will AI Destroy YouTube?". Useful for understanding C&S audience and language, not direct outreach targets but signal for the niche.

We're scraping all 49 for completeness. Outreach work will focus on guest episodes.

## Method note — rate-limit safety

Per the `youtube-transcript` skill: YouTube's timed-text endpoint hard-blocks 5+ parallel caption fetches from a single IP for 6–24h. This batch ran **sequential, one video at a time, with 3s sleep between calls**. Total runtime ~10–15 minutes. No retries on failure; if any single video blocks, we stop and resume tomorrow.
