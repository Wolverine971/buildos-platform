## Why this list is special

The Creator Support format is built around one mechanic: a real mid-tier creator brings a real workflow problem, and Colin & Samir solve it on camera in 15–30 minutes. **Every featured creator is a publicly hand-raised user.** They went on a podcast to admit they need help — they will not be defensive about a tool that helps with the exact problem they just discussed on the show.

These are NOT cold strangers. They are:

1. Self-identified as needing help.
2. On-air saying exactly what their problem is.
3. Already part of the Colin & Samir / Creator Support orbit.
4. Recently hyped (post-episode lift = fresh attention).

That's the cleanest outreach environment we can manufacture.

## Patterns observed across the 13 transcripts

Things that show up _repeatedly_ across the guest sessions — these are the BuildOS thesis being said by 13 creators in their own words:

1. **"Every project I start from zero."** (Tony, paraphrased by Colt, paraphrased by Kelly, paraphrased by Adrien, paraphrased by James.)
2. **"I'm the bottleneck."** (Mitch verbatim. Implied by Jerome, Bryce, Amanda.)
3. **"I want to be doing X every day but admin / business eats me."** (Amanda verbatim. Mitch on operational side. Bryce on workflow.)
4. **"I need a repeatable format."** (Tony, Kelly, Adrien, James, Colt, Tom — six of thirteen explicitly asked for this.)
5. **"My business is two parallel pipelines."** (Amanda: channel + brand. Mitch: Steep Streets + shorts + ride product. Peter: classes + YouTube + IG. James: short-form + long-form. Tom: band + show + Spotify.)
6. **"I do this in my head and it eats time."** (Jerome's 6-hour frame comments. Mitch's brand-deck homework that doesn't exist. Bryce's hooks-before-filming that he keeps abandoning.)

Every guest had at least 2 of these 6. **The shape is uniform.** That's the BuildOS wedge.

## C&S's recurring prescriptions (homework that needs a home)

What C&S kept assigning to guests as homework — each is BuildOS-shaped work:

- **Audience-Value worksheet** (every guest got this).
- **Identity-Emotion-Action exercise** (Mitch, Jerome, Suzie explicitly).
- **Niche analysis** (Bryce, Adrien) — screenshot every video in your niche with M+ views.
- **16 thumbnail frameworks** worksheet (Colt, Adrien, Tom).
- **Creator vision worksheet** (Amanda) — 16 questions financial/lifestyle/content.
- **50-in-5 / brainstorm 100 ideas with a constraint** (Kelly).
- **"Why not me" / Men's Health headline exercise** (Jerome).
- **Build a brand deck** (Mitch).
- **Origin-story video** (Bryce, Suzie).

If BuildOS shipped a "Creator Support Homework Pack" — pre-built project templates for each of these worksheets, instantiable per channel — every one of these creators is a warm intro.
