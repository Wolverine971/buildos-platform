**Before sending — sweep:**

- Replace `buildos.com` with whatever landing-page URL you actually want them on (e.g. `buildos.com/landing-v2`).
- Each email is plain text — no signature block, no banner image, no HTML, no UTM-laden link unless you want one.

## Reply-handling notes

When someone bites:

- **First reply within 24h.** Don't make them wait. Even a "great — give me 10 min to spin up a free seat for you" is fine.
- **Send a Loom, not a calendar link.** Walk through how their actual workflow maps to a BuildOS project (3-5 min, no slides). The Loom is the demo.
- **Don't ask for testimonials yet.** Get them using it first. Testimonials are for the local-creator-testimonial-hunt campaign, which is separate.
- **If they decline:** "Totally fair — if it ever shifts, my door's open." Don't follow up more than once.
