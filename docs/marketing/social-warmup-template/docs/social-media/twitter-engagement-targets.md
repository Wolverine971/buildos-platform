### Engagement Rules

1. **Be first** - Reply within 30 mins of post for max visibility
2. **Add value** - Don't just agree, extend the thought
3. **Be human** - Use your real experiences
4. **No hard sell** - Product mention only when genuinely relevant
5. **Ask questions** - Opens conversation threads
6. **Reference their content** - Show you actually follow them

### Voice Reminders

- lowercase casual. fragments fine.
- lead with observation, not "I think..."
- be direct. cut filler words.
- no "love this" or "great post"
- use "shameless plug" framing when mentioning your product

## Account Discovery Criteria

When scanning Following feed, flag accounts if they:

- Post about topics relevant to your niche
- Have 1K-100K followers (engagement sweet spot)
- Post consistently (multiple times per week)
- Have good engagement ratios
- Are builders/founders/practitioners (not just commentators)
- Write in a voice that resonates with your audience
