### Build the Target List (60-90 min)

- [ ] **LinkedIn search (30 min)** -- Search Posts, filter to past month:

    - [ ] `revising my manuscript`

    - [ ] `scattered notes writing`
    - [ ] `book project`
    - [ ] Search People for `writing coach`
    - [ ] For each hit: check process posts, recent activity, follower count, visible pain

- [ ] **X search (20 min)** -- Search recent:
    - [ ] `writing a book AND (scattered OR notes OR chaos OR overwhelmed)`
    - [ ] `manuscript revision AND (tools OR system OR organize)`
    - [ ] `#amwriting AND (too many tabs OR lost track OR scattered)`
    - [ ] `series bible AND (need OR help OR looking for)`

- [ ] **Instagram search (10 min)** -- Explore hashtags:
    - [ ] `#writingprocess`
    - [ ] `#writersofinstagram` (filter by recency)
    - [ ] `#amrevising`

    - [ ] Look for organizational struggle, not aesthetic desk photos

- [ ] **Reddit scan (15 min)** -- Search recent posts:
    - [ ] r/writing -- "organize" or "scattered" or "too many tools"
    - [ ] r/WritingAdvice -- "manuscript management"
    - [ ] r/fantasywriters -- "world building organization"
    - [ ] r/selfpublish -- "project management"
    - [ ] Find their LinkedIn/X from profile or portfolio links

- [ ] **Existing network (5 min)** -- Scan LinkedIn connections and X follows for writers, coaches, editors you already know

- [ ] **Fill in the list** -- 20 rows total:
    - [ ] 10 in Bucket A (direct user leads -- working writers)
    - [ ] 5 in Bucket B (audience-multiplier leads -- coaches, editors, newsletters)
    - [ ] 5 in Bucket C (research/proof leads -- people describing the pain publicly)
    - [ ] Each row: Name, Platform, Profile URL, Bucket, Last Relevant Post, Pain Signal, Status

### Record the Hero Asset (30 min)

- [ ] Open BuildOS, open a blank brain dump
- [ ] Have scattered source material nearby (text file, notes, notebook)
- [ ] Start screen recording (QuickTime, Loom, or OBS) with voice narration
- [ ] **Record the scene:**
    - [ ] (5-10s) Show the messy starting state, say "I have a chapter I am revising. The notes are in three places..."
    - [ ] (30-60s) Type or paste a realistic messy author brain dump (chapter issues, continuity problems, scattered notes, revision confusion)
    - [ ] (20-40s) Show what BuildOS produces -- walk through structured output, point out revision tasks, continuity flags, dependencies
    - [ ] (10-15s) Close with: "This is what I mean by a thinking environment. The project starts remembering with you." Stop. No pitch.
- [ ] **Do NOT:** show onboarding/signup, mention AI, use polished voiceover tone, add a signup CTA

### Extract the Reusable Assets (10 min)

- [ ] Screenshot the messy input on the left, structured output on the right (before/after pair)
- [ ] Cut a 15-30 second clip of just the transformation moment (dump in, structure out)

### Write the LinkedIn Post (20-30 min)

- [ ] **Hook** (line 1): Specific writer observation, not generic. e.g., "My chapter revision notes live in four places. I fixed that yesterday."
- [ ] **Scene** (lines 2-6): Describe the problem -- scattered revision notes, continuity issues, losing track of your own project. Use words like "muddy middle," "scattered ideas," "lost track."
- [ ] **Transformation** (lines 7-10): What happened, not what BuildOS is. "I dumped the whole mess into one place. Two minutes later I had a revision plan with the continuity issues flagged."
- [ ] **CTA** (lines 11-12): "If your draft is alive but your system is not, reply or DM me. I will tear down your workflow for free."
- [ ] **Do NOT say:** "AI-powered," "productivity tool," "check out BuildOS," "launch," "announcing." Do NOT tag people.

## Day 7 -- April 17 (Thursday): CHECK-IN

- [ ] Fill out the full scorecard in the campaign brief
- [ ] Answer the 5 post-campaign questions:
    1. What exact words did people use when they responded?
    2. Which bucket (A, B, or C) produced the best responses?
    3. What was the most common objection or hesitation?
    4. Did anyone describe a workflow that surprised you or that BuildOS does not handle?

- [ ] Apply decision criteria:
    - [ ] **CONTINUE** if: 3+ meaningful conversations, or 1+ teardown request, or pain thesis confirmed by language, or writers (not just general network) engaged
    - [ ] **NARROW** if: responses came from a different sub-segment, or CTA didn't convert despite attention
    - [ ] **PIVOT** if: no writer-specific traction, polite but uninterested replies, pain thesis felt vague, couldn't find 10 Bucket A matches
    - [ ] **KILL** if: couldn't ship asset, couldn't find list, zero qualified responses, author wedge feels wrong
