# Adjacent Creators — Top Performers (last 12 months)

> Source data for the [Data-Driven Topic Validation tasker](../strategy/data-driven-topic-validation-2026-05-15.md). Step 2 output.
> Companion analysis (clusters + BuildOS lens): [`./adjacent-creators-topic-clusters-2026-05-15.md`](./adjacent-creators-topic-clusters-2026-05-15.md).

## Methodology + caveats

- 5 parallel research agents pulled top ~20 pieces per creator across each one's active platforms (YouTube, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Substack, blog, podcast appearances) between 2025-05-15 and 2026-05-15.
- **Direct view counts on individual videos/tweets are mostly opaque to scrapers.** Where exact metrics weren't available, agents triangulated using third-party trackers (socialblade, youtubers.me), creators' own annual reviews, secondary citations, and re-share signal. Marked `metric_unavailable` where unknown.
- A handful of borderline entries (just outside the window) are included with a `*` and called out — kept only when they anchor a clear in-window pattern.
- Naval's table includes 12-month re-viral moments of older tweets, clearly marked — for him, recirculation is more meaningful than fresh posts.
- Khe's RadReads legacy newsletter is dormant in the window; his active publication is `khemaridh.substack.com`. Substack like-counts are visible; LinkedIn engagement is not.
