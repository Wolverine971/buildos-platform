# The two-headed machine

**Lane:** Brand (manifesto energy) · **Format:** LinkedIn long-form or blog → cut-downs · **Status:** queued

**Hook:** "One machine eats your thinking twice: once on the way in, once on the way out."

**The idea:** The apex-villain manifesto. Head 1 — the feed: decides what enters your mind; algorithm-shaped thoughts. Head 2 — the stateless chat box: takes your best thinking and forgets it; a meeting you can never leave. The counter isn't rage, it's relief: your own workstation, chosen input, compounding output. This is the sharpest single statement of what BuildOS is against and therefore what it's for.

**Source:** Worldbuilding map §3.07 (ratified 7/02). Distinct from idea 01 (roster/comedy angle); this is the serious flagship framing. Don't ship both close together.
