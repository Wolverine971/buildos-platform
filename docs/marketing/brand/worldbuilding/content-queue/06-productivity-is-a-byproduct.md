# Productivity is a byproduct

**Lane:** Brand (essay energy; personal version possible) · **Format:** LinkedIn post; strong blog candidate · **Status:** queued

**Hook:** "You can't chase productivity, the same way you can't chase happiness."

**The idea:** People who chase happiness directly stay miserable; happiness is the byproduct of pursuing your passions and doing good work. Productivity has the same shape: chase it directly and you get productivity theater — new apps, new systems, no progress. You're only productive when your thinking is clear. So don't pursue productivity; pursue clarity. Get into a thinking environment, get the mess out of your head, and productivity shows up as the byproduct. BuildOS named at the close.
