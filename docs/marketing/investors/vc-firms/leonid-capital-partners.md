## Investment Thesis

**"Private Credit for Government Contractors"**

Leonid Capital Partners delivers private credit solutions for national security tech and government contractors. They are a specialized credit provider for businesses contracting with the federal government for highly technical engagements in software, biotech, and engineering. As an official Department of Defense Trusted Capital Provider, they offer advance financing on all government contracts, including SBIR and STTRs.

### Key Differentiator:

This is **debt financing**, not equity investment. They provide working capital and credit facilities, not venture capital in the traditional sense.

## Why Leonid Capital Is NOT Right for BuildOS

### Fundamental Mismatch:

1. **Debt, Not Equity**: Leonid provides credit facilities and loans, not venture capital
2. **Government Contractor Focus**: Requires existing government contracts
3. **Revenue Requirement**: Finances companies with government revenue
4. **Not Startup Stage**: Designed for established contractors, not early-stage startups
5. **Wrong Capital Type**: BuildOS needs equity investment, not debt financing

### What They Actually Do:

- Advance financing on government contracts
- Working capital for SBIR/STTR recipients
- Credit facilities for defense contractors
- Acquisition financing for government services companies

## When Leonid WOULD Be Relevant:

Leonid could be relevant for BuildOS in the future if:

1. BuildOS wins government contracts (SBIR, STTR, defense procurement)
2. BuildOS needs working capital to fulfill government orders
3. BuildOS acquires a government contracting business
4. BuildOS has significant government revenue to finance
