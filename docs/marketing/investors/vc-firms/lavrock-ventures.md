## Investment Thesis

**"Enterprise Software, Cybersecurity, and National Security"**

Lavrock Ventures focuses on B2B technology companies, particularly in enterprise software, data, deep tech, and national security sectors. They invest in founders solving critical problems at the intersection of software, deep tech, and national security. Based right outside Washington, D.C., they are enterprise technology investors first and foremost.

### Dual-Use Focus:

They invest in both commercial and national security markets because these markets are increasingly converging. Technologies that start in the defense sector often find commercial applications, and vice versa.
