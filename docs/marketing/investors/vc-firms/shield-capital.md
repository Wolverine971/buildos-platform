## Investment Thesis

**"Technologies That Matter"**

Shield Capital invests in early-stage companies building technologies that matter in artificial intelligence, autonomy, cybersecurity, and space. They focus on the intersection of national security and commercial technology, particularly in dual-use sectors.

### Strategic Partnership:

Shield Capital has a formal strategic partnership with L3Harris Technologies, designed with L3Harris CEO Chris Kubasik. This provides portfolio companies access to a strategic partner while avoiding traditional downsides of corporate venture funds.
