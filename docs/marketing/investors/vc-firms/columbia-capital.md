## Investment Thesis

**"Picks and Shovels for the Digital Economy"**

Columbia Capital focuses on digital infrastructure, enterprise technology, and mobility. They employ a "picks and shovels" approach to supporting the digital economy, investing in mobile networks, infrastructure underlying public and private networks, and providers building hyperscale and enterprise compute environments. Their team has invested in and built unique digital infrastructure assets for over two decades, including data centers, towers, small cells, and fiber networks.
