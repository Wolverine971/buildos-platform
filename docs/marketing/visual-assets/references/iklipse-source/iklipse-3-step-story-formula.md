## Beat skeleton

Hook → the failure (3 bullets) → mechanism ("people engage with questions") + name the trio → **3× [Step + Quick-rule + "the viewer should ask: ___"]** → formula (trio → Attention + Engagement) → checklist → comment-bait CTA.

Same house formula as Weird AI / AI Trap (**Trio → Formula → Checklist → keyword CTA**), but with two strong reusable devices:

1. **"The viewer should ask: \_\_\_"** — each step is anchored to the literal question it plants in the viewer's head (Who is this? / What's going on? / What happens next?).
2. **"Engage with questions, not images"** mechanism slide.

## Highest-value extractables (this is the best one so far for us)

- **Character + Conflict + Curiosity** → 9takes: every personality take has **a Character (the type), a Conflict (the situation/the wound), and Curiosity (what they're NOT saying).** This is a ready-made 9takes formula slide.
- **"The viewer should ask" questions** → reframe as "When you read someone, you should be asking: _Who are they being right now? What are they protecting? What aren't they saying?_"
- **"Curiosity outperforms clarity"** + "show enough to understand, hide enough to make them wonder" → this IS the give-first mechanic (show one Nine, withhold the other eight until you contribute).
