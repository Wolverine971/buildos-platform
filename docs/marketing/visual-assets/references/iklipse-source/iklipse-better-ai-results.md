## 9takes translation

"**How to actually understand someone.**" Reframe: _"Your read is only as good as what you pay attention to."_ Then a ✗/✓ diptych:

- **Shallow inputs:** ✗ their words only · ✗ your assumptions · ✗ one moment · ✗ the stereotype · ✗ no context.
- **Real inputs:** ✓ tone + body · ✓ their stated motive · ✓ a pattern over time · ✓ their actual type · ✓ the triggering situation.
  The test: _"Before you judge — would your read still hold if you were wrong about their type?"_ CTA: "Comment your type." The ✗/✓ format is one of the most efficient teaching layouts in the set.
