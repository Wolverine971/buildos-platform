# BuildOS Brain Dump Tutorial — Script

## Working narration

Brain dump what’s on your mind and what’s going on with your project.

“I’m starting a small podcast called Lantern House. Six episodes, three guests, cover art, a launch date, and the first tasks.”

BuildOS turns that brain dump into a shared project and creates the work around it: tasks, a goal, a plan, and a document. Now you and the BuildOS agent are on the same page.

Tomorrow, when something changes, open the same project and say what changed.

“The guest list changed. Move the launch date and update the next tasks for Lantern House.”

BuildOS tracks the change and builds on top of your previous work.

Connect OpenClaw, Claude, and ChatGPT to the Lantern House project. One shared project gives you and every connected agent the same context. Keep moving forward.

## On-screen copy

1. **Brain dump what’s going on with your project.**
2. **BuildOS organizes it.**

4. **Now you and the BuildOS agent are on the same page.**
5. **Tomorrow, say what changed. BuildOS tracks it.**
6. **Connect your agents to the Lantern House project.**
7. **One shared project. Keep moving forward.**

## Delivery notes

- Calm, practical, and direct.
- Both brain dumps type directly inside the real mobile agentic-chat composer.
- After each brain dump, the animated Brain Bolt pulses as BuildOS processes the change.
- Creation and update receipts pop in individually.
- In the tomorrow scene, the “Create launch checklist” card is outlined and labeled “Task updated.”
- The video remains understandable with narration muted.
