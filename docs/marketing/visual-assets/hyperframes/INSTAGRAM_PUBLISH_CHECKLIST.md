# Instagram Reel Publish Checklist

Use this after the final silent master is rendered. The default organic workflow adds music in Instagram so the account can use the currently available licensed library and select the exact segment in context.

## Choose the audio path

### Organic Reel — default

- Upload the silent master.
- Add a track with Instagram’s Music tool.
- Prefer instrumental or low-lyric audio so the on-screen story remains dominant.
- Set music low enough that future voiceover or interface sounds would remain clear; the piece must still work muted.
- Remember that licensed tracks can later be muted if rights agreements change.

### Reusable or potentially boosted Reel

- Do not use copyrighted/Instagram-licensed music in the master.
- Use original audio or a track you own or have licensed for commercial reuse.
- Keep the silent master permanently.
- Export a separately named audio version; never overwrite the silent master.

Meta currently requires boosted Reels to be full-screen 9:16 and under 90 seconds, and says Reels with copyrighted music cannot be boosted. Official references: [audio types and muting](https://www.facebook.com/help/329208821595430), [adding music in Reels](https://www.facebook.com/help/664508917489819/), [boosting requirements](https://www.facebook.com/help/instagram/570215404599013).

## BuildOS music direction

For Inkprint proof stories:

- instrumental;
- restrained documentary/indie texture;
- approximately 80–105 BPM;
- audible first beat within the opening half-second;
- a subtle change or pulse near the first dark-mode interruption;
- no inspirational-corporate crescendo;
- no glitchy “AI future” sound design;
- no lyrics competing with the hook.

For the Cold Manuscript pilot, audition music whose energy changes around 3.2 seconds and again near the real product receipt around 10 seconds. Do not recut the story merely to chase a song unless the hook remains intact.

## Upload

- [ ] Use the final 1080×1920 silent master.
- [ ] Confirm the duration and first frame in the Instagram preview.
- [ ] Add music using the chosen audio path.
- [ ] Preview once with sound and once muted.
- [ ] Choose a cover from the QA frames; preview both profile-grid and feed crops.
- [ ] Keep the hook and BuildOS mark inside the crop shown by Instagram.
- [ ] Paste the approved caption.
- [ ] Add alt text when the interface provides it.
- [ ] Add collaborator only when account roles and permissions are already decided.
- [ ] Select Trial Reel only when it is the campaign’s declared experiment.
- [ ] Do not boost until organic performance and music rights are understood.

## Before tapping Share

- [ ] This is a real product receipt or clearly labeled internal example.
- [ ] No private tabs, names, emails, calendars, or customer data are visible.
- [ ] The caption does not imply unsupported automation.
- [ ] The CTA matches the campaign destination and UTM.
- [ ] The final question is easy for the intended creator to answer.
- [ ] The account posting and any Collab partner match the channel-role decision.

## After publishing

- [ ] Save the post URL.
- [ ] Log the URL, format, proof asset, CTA, and UTM content ID in the campaign scorecard.
- [ ] Stay available for replies during the first response window.
- [ ] Record qualified Writer language verbatim.
- [ ] Review reach, non-follower distribution, shares/sends, saves, profile visits, DMs/clicks, and activated users at the declared decision window.
- [ ] Choose keep, iterate hook, iterate proof, iterate handoff, or kill.
