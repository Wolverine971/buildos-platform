## Reply Guidelines Reminder (DJ Voice)

### The 3 Rules (Every Reply Must Pass)

1. **Can I visualize it?** - Use specific details, not abstract concepts
2. **Can I falsify it?** - Is this your real experience? Could you defend it if challenged?
3. **Can nobody else say this?** - Use your marines background, solo founder journey, ADHD brain

### Voice Principles

- Lowercase casual. perfect grammar not required.
- Short punchy sentences. fragments fine.
- Start with observation or reaction, not "I think..."
- Be direct. cut filler words.
- Longer replies are fine when you have real substance

### DO

- Lead with insight, not agreement
- Reference your specific experience (marines, building, ADHD)
- Get visual and specific - details make it real
- Be self-aware about plugs ("shameless plug incoming", "this is literally why i built...")
- State what smart people are already doing (insider knowledge)
- Use playful language when it fits ("slurping up that yummy context")
- Frame problems as "most people" experiencing what you've solved

### DON'T

- Copy-paste - these are starting points, personalize
- Reply to all at once (looks spammy)
- Just say "great post!" or generic praise
- Open with "love this" or "this resonated"
- Sound like AI wrote it
- Be preachy or lecture-y ("here's what's actually expensive...")
- Use generic thought leader phrases ("obsessed with X over Y")
