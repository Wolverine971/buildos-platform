## The Philosophy: Interesting Guy + Cheerleader

> **NOT a thought leader.** Everyone is doing that. It's overdone and inauthentic.
> **The goal:** Interesting person with interesting things to say, OR genuine supporter.

### Two Modes of Engagement

**Mode 1: Add Value (When You Have Something)**

- Share specific experience or insight
- Lead with curiosity, not authority
- Authentic and conversational - Instagram rewards real
- Teammate energy, not teaching

**Mode 2: Cheerleader (When You Don't)**

- Genuine excitement for their work/win
- Specific praise - what exactly impressed you
- Curious questions about their journey
- Supportive energy - "we're all figuring this out"

## Voice (Instagram-Specific)

Instagram sits between Twitter's casual brevity and LinkedIn's professional depth.

- **Authentic above all** - Instagram's culture penalizes corporate/polished vibes
- **Conversational, not performative** - Write like you're texting a friend who shares your interests
- **Visual-first platform** - Comments should add to the visual story, not lecture
- **Emoji-friendly** (but don't overdo it) - 1-2 emojis per comment is natural here
- **Shorter than LinkedIn, more substance than Twitter** - 1-3 sentences is the sweet spot for comments
- **Personal > professional** - Instagram audiences want to know the human, not the founder

### Comment Tone

- More warm and personal than Twitter
- Less formal than LinkedIn
- Can use emojis naturally (not forced)
- Direct and real - no corporate speak
- Like leaving a note for a friend

### DO

- Reference specific things in their post/reel ("that part about X hit different")
- Ask genuine questions about their experience
- Share a quick relevant anecdote from your own journey
- Use natural emoji (fire, 100, brain, clap) when genuine
- Engage with Stories via replies and reactions

### DON'T

- Drop generic comments ("amazing!" "so true!" "fire content!")
- Write essays in comments (save depth for DMs or your own posts)
- Use hashtags in comments on other people's posts (looks spammy)
- Turn every comment into a BuildOS pitch
- Be overly professional/LinkedIn-brain on Instagram

## How to Plug BuildOS

**Be transparent and casual. Instagram audiences smell ads from a mile away.**

- "building something for this exact problem actually - @buildos"
- "this is literally why I started building buildos lol"
- Only when genuinely relevant - maybe 1 in 10 comments max
- Stories and Reels are better for product mentions than comments

---

## Comment Starters

**Good:**

- "yo this is so real - [specific thing that resonated]"
- "the part about [X] is exactly what I keep running into"
- "genuinely curious - how do you handle [specific aspect]?"
- "this is the content I needed today"
- "hell yes. [specific praise about what they shared]"

**Bad:**

- "amazing content!" (generic)
- "so inspiring!" (hollow)
- "check out my page!" (spammy)
- "most people don't realize..." (preachy)
- Just emojis with no words (low effort)

1. **Story replies** - Goes to DMs, creates real conversations, most personal

## Engagement Cadence

- **Story engagement**: Check and reply to Stories throughout the day (most personal)
- **Reel comments**: Prioritize Reels <1h old for maximum visibility
- **Feed comments**: 1-3 sentences, specific and genuine
- **Spacing**: 10-15 min between comments (Instagram is stricter on rate limiting)
- **Target**: 5-10 quality engagements per day
- **Best times**: 9-11 AM EST, 7-9 PM EST (Instagram peaks differ from LinkedIn/Twitter)
- **Reels timing**: Evenings and weekends tend to perform better

## Instagram vs Twitter vs LinkedIn

| Aspect               | Instagram                                   | Twitter                     | LinkedIn                     |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Tone**             | Warm, personal, authentic                   | Lowercase casual, punchy    | Professional but real        |
| **Comment length**   | 1-3 sentences                               | Short, sharp                | 2-4 sentences                |
| **Emoji use**        | Natural, expected                           | Minimal                     | Minimal                      |
| **Product mentions** | Rare, very casual                           | Soft plug OK                | Transparent mention OK       |
| **Audience**         | ADHD community, creators, gen Z/millennials | Indie hackers, builders, AI | Professionals, founders, VCs |
| **Discovery**        | Reels, Explore, hashtags                    | Search, topics, For You     | Feed, search, hashtags       |
| **Engagement style** | Personal, supportive, community             | Quick takes, reactions      | Thoughtful, nuanced          |
