# Local Creator Testimonial Hunt — 2026-05-11

**Status:** Active research
**Goal:** Find 10 creators in the DC / Baltimore / NYC corridor to serve as testimonial / case-study subjects for BuildOS.
**Owner:** DJ
**Target outcome:** 3–5 signed-on case studies within 60 days; 10 in flight.

---

## Strategy: Lead with Presuasion

Filter the entire creator universe down to **people who have already named the pain publicly**. That single filter removes ~90% of the market and gives us natural cold-open hooks ("saw your post about X").

We are not selling. We are giving:

- A workflow interview that positions them as the expert
- 90 days of BuildOS, free
- A finished piece of content they can use however they want

In exchange we get a testimonial / case-study artifact.

---

## Ideal Candidate Profile

A creator who fits **all** of:

- **Role:** author, YouTuber, course creator, or podcaster (multi-hyphenate strongly preferred)
- **Audience size:** 5K–50K (enough weight to matter, small enough they'll respond)
- **Public pain signal in last 90 days:** tool churn (Notion / Roam / Obsidian), "too many projects," second-brain frustration, AI workflow experimentation, overwhelm
- **Active output:** shipping something this quarter — book, course launch, podcast season, product
- **Location:** Baltimore, Annapolis, DC, Bethesda, NYC, or the corridor in between

---

## Presuasion Tiers

| Tier                      | Definition                                                                   |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1 — Warm**              | Posted the exact pain in last 30 days; mid-launch; openly building in public |
| **2 — Likely**            | Multi-hyphenate, audience match, has publicly used adjacent tools            |
| **3 — Cold but high-fit** | Fits the profile but no public pain signal yet                               |

Outreach starts **Tier 1 only**. Tier 2 is the second wave. Tier 3 is bench depth.

## Interview / Case-Study Structure

When a creator says yes, the call should produce both the testimonial and the content piece. Suggested 30-min structure:

1. **(5 min)** Their current setup — tools, workflows, pain points
2. **(10 min)** Walk through a real project they're shipping — what's hard about it
3. **(10 min)** Live brain dump into BuildOS — record this if they allow
4. **(5 min)** First reaction — quote-worthy moments

After: 90-day check-in scheduled at end of call. That's the testimonial moment.
