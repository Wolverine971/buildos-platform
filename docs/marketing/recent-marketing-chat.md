You’re running into a **real macro shift** happening right now:
People are moving from **AI curiosity → AI exhaustion**.

Early phase (2022–2024):

> “AI can do everything!”

Current phase (2025– ):

> “Please stop showing me another AI tool.”

That shift actually **creates an opportunity** for BuildOS if you position it correctly. The key is:

**Do not market BuildOS as an AI product.**

Market it as a **solution to chaos.**

---

# The Game Theory of AI Fatigue

### Players

1. **Users**
2. **AI startups**
3. **Big tech (OpenAI, Google, etc.)**
4. **You / BuildOS**

### Current incentives

**Users want**

- Less complexity
- Fewer tools
- Less hype
- Real results

**AI startups want**

- To say “AI” everywhere
- To demo cool tricks

This creates a gap:

**Everyone is selling magic.
Nobody is selling relief.**

That’s where you win.

# The Secret Weapon: Demonstration Content

AI fatigue mostly comes from **promises**.

The antidote is **demonstration**.

Instead of telling people:

> “BuildOS uses AI to organize projects.”

Show:

```
Raw brain dump:

"I want to start a youtube channel
about military history and also
write a book and launch a podcast"

↓

BuildOS Output:

Project: Military History Channel
Tasks:
• research episode 1
• script outline
• record pilot

Project: Book
• outline chapters
• research sources
• draft intro
```

People instantly understand.

No AI explanation required.
