# Usability Evaluation And Quick Research

Use this draft when turning source material into the runtime `usability_quick_research` child skill.

## When to Use

- Planning quick research, usability tests, interviews, participant criteria, assumption checks, prototype tests, or synthesis.
- A UI decision needs user evidence rather than another internal opinion.
- Stakeholders are resisting research, relying on old research, or asking for validation after a decision is already made.

## Workflow

1. State the decision and bet size.
2. Name current beliefs, unknowns, and disconfirming evidence.
3. Separate discovery from evaluative questions.
4. Choose the smallest credible method for the current decision.
5. Define participant or proxy criteria and limitations.
6. Synthesize into decisions, design changes, risks, and next questions.
