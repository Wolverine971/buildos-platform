## Pruning Standard

Keep a source card only if it directly supports the cold email architecture, a child skill, a compliance gate, or a specialty mode. Remove source cards that are merely raw technical admin pages, generic deliverability troubleshooting, shallow book marketing pages, duplicate vendor posts, or old template material that would make the skill worse.

## System-Level Synthesis

The active corpus reinforces the master outreach flow: right person -> right moment -> right reason -> right offer -> right ask -> right follow-up. The sources now split into four useful jobs:

- Deliverability and compliance: launch gates and legal/provider boundaries.
- Compiler, taste, and sequence: inbox feel, package quality, and follow-up behavior.
- ICP, offer, and learning: campaign justification and market-learning discipline.
- Specialty modes: investor, recruiting, PR/podcast, and founder/creator standards.
