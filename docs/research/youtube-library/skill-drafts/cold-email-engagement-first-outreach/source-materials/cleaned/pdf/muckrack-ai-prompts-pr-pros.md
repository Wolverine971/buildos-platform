AI P RO MP TS FOR P R P R OS

AI can be a powerful tool for PR pros. Use these AI prompts to help generate

new ideas for pitches, blog posts, social media, research and more.

BEFO RE YOU GE T STAR T E D, D O N’T FO R GE T:

Be specific and add context whenever possible

Set the tone to generate the best results for your audience

Define a character limit to save time on creating variations

Avoid jargon, acronyms and slang

Be mindful of privacy since some AI tools record conversations

Test different variations to get relevant results

Review your results carefully for accuracy

Personalize it to make it your own

WO R DS TO GE T YOU STAR TE D

The verbs you include in your AI prompt make all the difference in getting the

results you want. Here are a few to get you started.

Start your prompt with one of these words:

Example: List 5 ideas for an email subject line about a new pet grooming service

• List • Describe • Answer

• Generate • Brainstorm • Outline

• Produce • Name • Write

• Propose • Develop • Argue

• Combine • Reduce • Summarize

• Elaborate • Clarify • Illustrate

• Refine • Synthesize • Predict

• Expand • Interpret
