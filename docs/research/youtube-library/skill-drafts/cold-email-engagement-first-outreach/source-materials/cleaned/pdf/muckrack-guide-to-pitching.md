Business and finance

Muck Rack’s State of Journalism report is a great place to learn about the overall journalism

trends that impact the industry and influence the PR world.

But journalists are a broad group with many different ways of working and differing feelings

about public relations professionals. Muck Rack created this pitching guide to provide

insights into how different groups of journalists work and how to best pitch them.

Each group of journalists discussed on the following pages have their own working styles,

preferred social platforms, pitching tendencies and more. This guide will first delve into the

data and figures that make each group unique. Then, we’ll hear insights directly from the

journalists themselves to add context to the data.

Over the next few pages, you’ll meet 19 journalists that cover a variety of beats including

healthcare, business & finance, technology, and lifestyle.
