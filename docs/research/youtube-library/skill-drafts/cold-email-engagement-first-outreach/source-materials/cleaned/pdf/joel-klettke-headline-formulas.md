Headline Formula Cheat Sheet

Your headline only has one job: get people to stop, sit up, pay attention, and keep

- Keep it short and sweet (10–12 words max, generally)

- Make it about your visitor/reader

- Steal from your voice of customer

- Match headline to customer journey

ALL of your content should also pass a “so what” test—especially your headline/sub-headline

It’s not enough to just make a statement and expect people to take it on faith. Ask yourself “so

what” and then answer how/why your headline matters to the person reading your page, blog
