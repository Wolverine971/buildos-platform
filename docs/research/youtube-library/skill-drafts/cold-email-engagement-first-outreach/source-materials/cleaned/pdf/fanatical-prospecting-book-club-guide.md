The Prospecting Pyramid

Knowing your objective for each prospecting call makes you more efficient because you

are able to build prospecting blocks and group your prospecting channel touches around

those objectives. This allows you to make more prospecting touches in less time. Top

performers organize their prospecting blocks to win with highly qualified prospects who

are in the buying window. There are four primary objectives of prospecting that will help

you know what to ask for and how to bridge to your prospect's problems.
