About Us
Higharc is a VC-backed startup that is changing how new homes are designed and built. Join a founding team who’ve shipped products for Autodesk, Electronic Arts, Nike, and Apple. We have raised a total of $83M with support from top-notch venture capital firms and more than 18 strategic investors—industry leaders in construction, building products manufacturing, and distribution.

We are seeking an experienced Senior Full Stack Software Engineer to evolve technical direction of our B2B platform’s integrations with customer systems, especially ERP platforms. This role requires not only strong engineering skills but also the architectural insight to design and guide robust, scalable integration patterns across a variety of customer environments—many of which have inconsistent API support..

Core Responsibilities
Design and implement integrations between our platform and customer ERP systems, including REST and other API-based approaches as well as non-standard integration patterns.

Shape the technical architecture to ensure stability, scalability, and long-term maintainability of our integration solutions.

Build and evolve customer-facing APIs and supporting backend services to enable seamless data exchange and automation.

Collaborate with product managers and customer success teams to understand integration requirements and translate them into technical solutions.

Establish high standards for code quality, testing, and monitoring of integration services.

Provide technical leadership and mentorship, helping the team navigate the complexity of heterogeneous enterprise systems.

Required Qualifications
Demonstrated experience designing and delivering integrations with ERP or other complex enterprise systems.

Strong proficiency in TypeScript/JavaScript and experience with our core stack (React, Node.js, Next.js, PostgreSQL).

Ability to define and drive architectural decisions for API design and enterprise integrations.

Proven track record of building and deploying scalable B2B or enterprise-facing applications.

Strong communication skills and comfort working with cross-functional teams and external stakeholders.

Portfolio of relevant integration or API projects (via GitHub, personal website, or equivalent).

While we've identified the core experience and skills required above, please still apply if you have more or different experience than this! We will use your previous experience and performance across the series of interviews to establish appropriate level within our organization in a fair and equitable way.

Working at Higharc
Higharc has been remote first since our founding in 2018. We offer flexible hours so you can do your best work without missing out on life. Higharc offers competitive salaries with significant equity, in a fast-growing, well-funded company.

Personal healthiness is an important value for us- we provide comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage, with unlimited PTO, and meaningful maternity/paternity leave to all U.S based employees that are full-time. You'll also have access to other big-company benefits such like short and long-term disability plans and a 401K. Haven't worked remotely before? We provide a stipend to create the ideal home office.
