Usage in Svelte Components

To enable dithering on your cards, use the dithered prop:

<Card dithered>
  <CardHeader dithered variant="gradient">
    <h2>My Card Title</h2>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardBody dithered>
    <p>Card content goes here</p>
  </CardBody>

  <CardFooter dithered>
    <button>Action</button>
  </CardFooter>
</Card>

Pro Tips:

  • Use dithered on headers with gradients for the best effect
  • Body dithering is very subtle - perfect for content areas
  • Footer dithering adds subtle texture to action areas
  • Mix and match: not all components need to be dithered
  • The effect is automatically adjusted for dark mode
