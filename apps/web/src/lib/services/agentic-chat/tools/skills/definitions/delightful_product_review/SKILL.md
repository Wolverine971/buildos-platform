# Delightful Product Review

Use this child when a product needs more emotional pull, not just less friction.

## When to Use

- Reviewing consumer experiences, onboarding, empty states, achievement moments, habit loops, or emotionally important product surfaces.
- The user asks for delight, surprise, motivation, memorability, charm, or "wow" moments.
- A calm-software lens would remove too much of the product's emotional energy.

## Workflow

1. Identify the functional motivator: what progress, control, saving, status, learning, or ease the user wants.
2. Identify the emotional motivator: what the user should feel at the key moment.
3. Map friction, payoff, and expectation gaps across the journey.
4. Propose delight only where the core job is already clear and reliable.
5. Score ideas by user value, brand fit, effort, accessibility risk, and whether the moment will still feel good on repeat use.

## Guardrails

- Do not use delight to cover broken basics, unclear flows, or slow performance.
- Do not add celebratory motion or copy where the user is stressed, time-constrained, or trying to leave.
- Pair with `calm_software_design_review` when the product may be over-stimulating.
