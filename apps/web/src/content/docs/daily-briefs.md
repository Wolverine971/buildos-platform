A BuildOS Daily Brief is an AI-generated project status update for your whole workspace. It turns active projects, tasks, blockers, recent changes, and calendar commitments into a short daily plan you can read in email, SMS, or the app.

The point is not another notification. It is a reliable answer to: what moved, what is blocked, what changed since last time, and what deserves attention today.

## What Daily Briefs are for

Daily Briefs give founders, operators, and project leads a project-aware planning surface without requiring a manual status meeting. Each brief is generated from your BuildOS project graph, so it reflects the tasks, goals, documents, milestones, and decisions already connected to your work.

Use Daily Briefs when you want to:

- Start the day with a focused plan instead of scanning every project.
- See blocked or stale work before it disappears.
- Catch recent project changes without reading every update.
- Turn priority work into calendar blocks through the agent.
- Keep momentum across projects even when you were away from the app.

## Types

- **Per-project briefs.** Deep-dive updates scoped to a single project.
- **Daily master briefs.** A rollup across every active project, with today's priorities and the thread that connects them.

## Delivery

- **In-app.** Always available on the [Briefs](/briefs) page, with history and generation status.
- **Email.** Delivered to the address on your account at your scheduled time.
- **SMS.** Opt in from profile preferences.
- **Push.** Available when browser/device notification preferences are enabled.

Mix and match. You can turn channels on and off per brief type.

## What a brief contains

Briefs are generated from your project graph, not from a generic task list. Each one typically includes:

- **Project status** — real progress, not a restated task list.
- **Priority actions** — what to focus on today or this week.
- **Blockers and open questions** — items that need a decision.
- **Recent context** — insights pulled from the latest dumps and updates.
- **Calendar context** — the commitments and task-derived time blocks that shape the day.
- **Strategic alignment** — how active projects ladder into the goals you named.

## How BuildOS generates a Daily Brief

BuildOS reads your active projects, related tasks, milestones, goals, documents, project changes, and known calendar items. It then generates project-level updates and rolls those into a master daily brief with the highest-leverage next actions.

The brief is grounded in your workspace memory, so it gets better as you add project context through brain dumps, voice notes, documents, task updates, and calendar planning.

## Act on it in chat

Tap a brief card on the dashboard and the agent opens with the brief loaded. Useful prompts:

- _"Turn today's priorities into calendar blocks."_
- _"Explain why this task is on today's brief."_
- _"What did I actually ship this week across all projects?"_
- _"Pick three things I should finish before Friday."_

Anything the agent creates from a brief chat — events, new tasks, document updates — is written back into your project graph.

## Cadence and honest pacing

You control the cadence. If a brief channel gets noisy, turn that channel off or change the schedule in brief settings. You can also tune brief cadence and notification preferences through the agent in a conversation — _"send briefs every other weekday"_ is a perfectly fine thing to ask for.

When engagement backoff is enabled, BuildOS can reduce brief frequency for inactive accounts and resume normal cadence when you return.
