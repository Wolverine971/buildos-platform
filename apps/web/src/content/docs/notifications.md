Notifications in BuildOS are multi-channel and per-event configurable.

## Channels

- **In-app.** Always on. The Notifications Center at [`/notifications`](/notifications) is the activity log.
- **Push.** Browser and mobile web push notifications.
- **Email.** Delivered to your account address.
- **SMS.** Opt in from profile preferences; powered by Twilio.

## Per-event preferences

Each notification type — daily briefs, task reminders, calendar events, system alerts — can be routed to any combination of channels. Configure it from the Notifications Center or profile preferences.

## The activity log

[`/notifications`](/notifications) is the single place to see every notification you've received, across every channel, in chronological order. Use it to audit what went out, replay something you missed, or turn a channel off if it's noisy.
