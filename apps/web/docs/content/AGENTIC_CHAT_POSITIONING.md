## Part 6: Naming Exploration

### The Challenge

"Agentic Chat" is technically accurate but:

- Overused in market
- Carries "autonomous agent" baggage
- Doesn't communicate the SCOPED nature
- Sounds like every other AI feature

### Naming Criteria

A good name should:

1. **Differentiate** - Not sound like every other AI feature
2. **Communicate scope** - Imply focus/precision, not generic
3. **Feel intelligent** - Convey understanding, not just response
4. **Be memorable** - Sticky, easy to reference
5. **Align with brand** - Fit BuildOS voice ("energetic realist")

#### 1. **"Scoped Chat"** (Recommended for Technical Audiences)

- **Why:** Precise, differentiating, implies boundaries
- **Tagline:** "Chat scoped to your work, not the world"
- **Risk:** May need explanation for non-technical users
- **Best for:** Power users, developers, technical marketing

#### 2. **"Project Lens"** (Recommended for Consumer Positioning)

- **Why:** Visual metaphor (lens = focus, zoom), memorable, brandable
- **Tagline:** "See your projects through conversation"
- **Risk:** Doesn't explicitly say "chat"
- **Best for:** Marketing, landing pages, consumer messaging

#### 3. **"Contextual Conversation"** (Recommended for Enterprise)

- **Why:** Professional, accurate, implies intelligence
- **Tagline:** "AI that understands your project context"
- **Risk:** Slightly generic
- **Best for:** Enterprise sales, formal documentation
